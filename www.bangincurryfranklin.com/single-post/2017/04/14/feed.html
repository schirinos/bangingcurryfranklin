If you haven’t heard the buzz, we are open! We officially came back Halloween weekend and were met with an amazing crowd of people. Before we knew it, we were sold out and the weekend had just begun! We know we were closed much longer than expected but we wanted to make sure everything was perfect for the roll out of our brand new truck! Thanks to a great truck wrap by
Brands Imaging, if you happen to see a giant Ben Franklin hanging out somewhere on Washington Ave then what are you waiting for? Stop by, say hi and grab a bite to eat!
Thanksgiving is just around the corner and we will be closed the following weekend November 24-November 25, 2017. This year was amazing to say the least. We each welcomed two new additions to our family over the summer and we cannot be more grateful for this great opportunity to grow our families while pursuing our dreams. We want to sincerely thank our friends and family and that includes our customers. Thank you for all your support that allows us to do what we love.
Winter is coming. It might already be here but you know what that means. Say goodbye to those evening strolls around the block to pick up your weekly dose of Friday Furikake Fries. But that doesn’t mean we’re going away again. It means we’re working on a mobile ordering system so you can order ahead and no more waiting in the cold! Stay tuned for updates in the weeks ahead.
